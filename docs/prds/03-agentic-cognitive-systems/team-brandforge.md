---

## 1. Purpose

Develop comprehensive brand identities from strategy through naming, visual identity, voice definition, and consistency auditing — producing brand guidelines that ensure coherent market presence across all touchpoints.

---

## 2. Team Composition

| # | Agent | Role |
|---|-------|------|
| 1 | BrandStrategist | Defines brand strategy including positioning, target audience, differentiation, brand promise, and emotional territory |
| 2 | NamingExpert | Generates and evaluates brand names against criteria of memorability, domain availability, trademark risk, and cultural sensitivity |
| 3 | VisualIdentityDesigner | Designs visual identity guidelines including color palette, typography, logo concepts, imagery style, and spatial rules |
| 4 | VoiceToneDefiner | Defines brand voice characteristics, tone variations by context, messaging frameworks, and content principles |
| 5 | BrandAuditor | Audits brand deliverables for internal consistency, competitive differentiation, and alignment with brand strategy |

---

## 3. Workflow

1. **BrandStrategist** receives the business context (from StartupSim or direct brief) and produces a brand strategy document with positioning, audience, and brand pillars.
2. **NamingExpert** generates candidate names aligned with the brand strategy, evaluates each against objective criteria, and recommends a shortlist.
3. **VisualIdentityDesigner** develops visual identity guidelines that express the brand strategy through color, typography, and design language.
4. **VoiceToneDefiner** defines the verbal identity — voice attributes, tone spectrum, messaging hierarchy, and sample copy.
5. **BrandAuditor** reviews all deliverables for coherence, flags inconsistencies, and produces a final brand health score with remediation notes.

---

## 4. Integration Points

- **StartupSim** — receives startup concepts that have passed simulation for brand development
- **DocsCrew** — brand voice and tone guidelines inform documentation style
- **PhoenixVC-Website** — brand assets are applied to web properties
