# PRD: StartupSim Agent Team

**Project:** StartupSim
**Owner:** Hans Jurgens Smit — PhoenixVC / Nexamesh AI
**Status:** Draft v0.1
**Date:** 8 March 2026
**Classification:** Internal — Cognitive Mesh Infrastructure

---

## 1. Purpose

Simulate the full lifecycle of a startup concept — from market analysis through business model design, financial modeling, competitive mapping, and pitch preparation — to stress-test viability before committing real resources.

---

## 2. Team Composition

| # | Agent | Role |
|---|-------|------|
| 1 | MarketAnalyzer | Analyzes market opportunity including TAM/SAM/SOM sizing, growth trends, timing signals, and customer segmentation |
| 2 | BusinessModelDesigner | Designs and iterates on business models using Business Model Canvas, identifying revenue streams, cost structures, and value propositions |
| 3 | FinancialModeler | Creates financial projections including revenue forecasts, burn rate analysis, runway calculations, and break-even scenarios |
| 4 | CompetitorMapper | Maps the competitive landscape identifying direct/indirect competitors, their strengths/weaknesses, market positioning, and defensibility gaps |
| 5 | PitchCrafter | Generates investor pitch deck content including narrative arc, key metrics, team positioning, and ask structure |
| 6 | RiskAssessor | Identifies business risks across market, technical, regulatory, financial, and team dimensions, with mitigation strategies |

---

## 3. Workflow

1. **MarketAnalyzer** ingests the startup concept and produces a market opportunity assessment with sizing, trends, and customer personas.
2. **BusinessModelDesigner** takes the market assessment and designs 2-3 candidate business models with unit economics.
3. **CompetitorMapper** maps the competitive landscape and identifies positioning opportunities and threats for each business model.
4. **FinancialModeler** builds financial projections for the recommended business model across conservative, base, and optimistic scenarios.
5. **RiskAssessor** reviews all outputs and produces a risk register with severity ratings and mitigation plans.
6. **PitchCrafter** synthesizes all analyses into pitch-ready content with a compelling narrative.

---

## 4. Integration Points

- **BrandForge** — startup concepts that pass simulation are handed to BrandForge for brand development
- **WealthMind** — financial models feed into portfolio-level analysis
- **StakeholderSim** — simulation outputs can be tested against stakeholder reaction models
