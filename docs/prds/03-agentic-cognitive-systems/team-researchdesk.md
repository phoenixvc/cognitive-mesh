---

## 1. Purpose

ResearchDesk is a research and analysis team that evaluates emerging technologies, analyzes competitor approaches, mines codebase patterns, and reviews relevant academic and industry literature. The team provides evidence-based insights that inform architectural decisions, technology adoption, and strategic roadmap planning.

---

## 2. Team Composition

| # | Agent | Role |
|---|-------|------|
| 1 | TechRadarAnalyst | Evaluates emerging technologies, frameworks, and platforms for relevance, maturity, and potential adoption within the Cognitive Mesh ecosystem |
| 2 | CompetitorAnalyzer | Analyzes competitor products, architectures, and strategies to identify differentiation opportunities and potential threats |
| 3 | PatternMiner | Identifies recurring design patterns, anti-patterns, and code quality trends across the codebase to surface systemic improvements |
| 4 | LiteratureReviewer | Reviews academic papers, industry reports, and technical blogs on AI/ML, multi-agent systems, and cognitive architectures to extract actionable insights |

---

## 3. Workflow

1. **Scan**: TechRadarAnalyst continuously monitors the technology landscape for relevant innovations in AI frameworks, infrastructure tooling, and development practices.
2. **Analyze**: CompetitorAnalyzer researches competitor ecosystems, identifying their strengths, weaknesses, and strategic direction.
3. **Mine**: PatternMiner performs static analysis across the Cognitive Mesh codebase to detect recurring patterns, technical debt clusters, and improvement opportunities.
4. **Review**: LiteratureReviewer synthesizes findings from academic and industry publications relevant to current and planned capabilities.
5. **Synthesize**: The team consolidates findings into a structured research brief with recommendations, risk assessments, and priority rankings.
6. **Advise**: Research briefs are delivered to RoadmapCrew and StackSelect teams to inform planning and technology selection decisions.

---

## 4. Integration Points

- **RoadmapCrew**: Provides technology assessments and competitive intelligence to inform roadmap prioritization and feature planning.
- **StackSelect**: Delivers technology evaluations with maturity scores, community health metrics, and adoption risk profiles for stack decisions.
- **cognitive-mesh**: PatternMiner analyzes the core codebase for architectural pattern adherence and improvement opportunities.
- **All repositories**: PatternMiner scans across repositories to identify cross-cutting concerns and reusable abstractions.
